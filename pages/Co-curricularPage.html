    And Learnings
    - i am an enthusiast cyclist. My dream ride was Pune to Kanyakumari, 1600 kms - 10 Days in total which I could complete in December of 2021.
    It was an enriching holistic experience overall. That ride taught me the power of consistency and how we can accomplish great things if we tackle one thing at a time and do it consistently over an extended period of time.
    - This ride also taught me the importance of team work. We can surely do things faster if we work alone, but with a great team we can sustain it for longer time and the whole team gets further ahead than any single person can get.
